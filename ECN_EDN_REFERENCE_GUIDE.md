## 1. ELSEVIER-MASSON — Collection des Collèges

Official website: https://www.elsevier-masson.fr

Elsevier-Masson publishes the official "Collection des Collèges" — the reference
textbooks written by the French Medical College of each specialty. These are the
most authoritative references for ECN/EDN preparation.

### Key collection features:
- **Collection des Collèges** (blue covers) — the official college textbooks
- **Abrégés** — concise review books
- **ECN Pillules / EDN Pillules** — quick revision cards
- **Références ECN/EDN** — exam-focused books
- Online access via **EM-Consulte** (digital platform)

## 3. S-ÉDITIONS (Éditions S)

Official website: https://www.s-editions.com

S-Éditions is a newer publisher specializing in EDN-format references.
They focus on the new EDN exam format (Épreuves Dématérialisées Nationales)
that replaced ECN in 2023-2024.

### Key series:
- **Référentiels EDN** — comprehensive EDN references
- **Fiches EDN** — summary notes for EDN
- **Annales EDN** — past EDN exam papers

### Known products:
- Référentiel EDN — Médecine Intensive
- Référentiel EDN — Urgences
- Fiches EDN — Cardiologie
- Annales EDN 2024

## 4. DECARRE

Official website: https://www.decarre.fr

Decarre publishes revision manuals and exam prep books for medical students.

### Key series:
- **Manuels de Révision ECN**
- **Annales ECNi**
- **QCM ECN/EDN**

## 5. STUDYRAMA

Official website: https://www.studyrama.com

Studyrama publishes general revision guides including medical exam preparation.

### Key series:
- **Grand Oral** — preparation for the oral exam
- **Concours Médecine** — entrance exam prep
- **Révisions ECN/EDN**

## Notes

- Prices listed are approximate and may vary
- Most textbooks are also available in digital (PDF/ebook) format
- "Collection des Collèges" by Elsevier-Masson is the most comprehensive
- Vernazobres-Grégo's "Référentiels" are more concise and exam-focused
- S-Éditions is a newer player focusing specifically on the new EDN format
- After 2023, most publishers updated their content for the new EDN exam format
